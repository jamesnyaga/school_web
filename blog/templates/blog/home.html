Friquently asked Questions

Parents can access their child’s grades and academic progress through the online parent portal. Grades are updated regularly, and you can also view comments from teachers on your child’s performance. If you do not have access, please contact the school administration to set up your account.

To enroll a new student, you need to provide a completed application form, a copy of the birth certificate, previous school records, and proof of residency. Additional documents, such as vaccination records, may be required depending on the grade level.

The school operates from 8:00 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday through Friday. We recommend that students arrive at least 10 minutes before the start of classes to prepare for the day.

You can contact your child’s teacher via email or through the parent portal available on our website. In case of urgent matters, you may also call the school office, and they will facilitate communication with the teacher.

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities, including sports (soccer, basketball, swimming), arts (drama, music, painting), and academic clubs (science club, debate team, chess club). Students can sign up for these activities at the beginning of each term.
